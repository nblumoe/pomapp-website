Increase your productivity with a breeze and get time tracking for free! Our feature set covers the whole awesomeness of the PomodoroTechnique:

Easy to use Pomodoro timer

The timer is the hear of the PomodoroTechnique. Our timer is easy to use and does nice ticking and alarm sounds. Of course you can mute it, if you want to.

Create and prioritize tasks on the fly

Whenever a new task comes up to you, it is easy to store it. Just type in the task and hit enter. This way you do not loose focus, when you have to create a new task while working on another one.

Prioritizing tasks and moving them between the different lists works intuitively with Drag&Drop. Again, PomodoroEasy gets out of you way!

Track interruptions

When working on a task you will have to track interruptions. No problem: It's only one click! Of course there is support for internal and external interruptions.

Get reports of tracked time and distractions

To get the best out of the PomodoroTechnique in the longer term, it's great to have some analytics of your tasks. See if you could reduce the number of interruptions. Get reports about tracked time and see how you improve in estimating time needed for tasks.

Mobile and 'out of the way' support

PomodoroEasy aims at great user experience for small windows and on mobile devices. Thus you do not need a big window for it, wasting space on your desktop. Also, why not just use it on your smartphone or tablet besides your computer?
